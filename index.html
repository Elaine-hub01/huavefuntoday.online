How to Play Monster Survivors

Getting started with Monster Survivors is easy! Simply click the play button above to launch the game in your browser. Use your mouse or touch controls to move your character and aim at incoming monsters. Your character will automatically attack nearby enemies, but positioning and timing are crucial for survival.

Collect experience points and power-ups dropped by defeated monsters to upgrade your abilities. Choose your upgrades wisely, as different combinations can lead to unique playstyles and strategies. The key to success is balancing offensive capabilities with defensive positioning.

As you progress, you'll encounter boss monsters and special events that provide additional challenges and rewards. Stay alert, keep moving, and never let your guard down in this relentless battle for survival!
